PIJIN

Born (1995, Vilnius) and raised in Klaipeda city. Since 2022, curating activities of the Artojas Street Art Park in Klaipeda (www.artojo.lt). Pijus Ceikauskas is a multidisciplinary artist and illustrator known as Pijin. Sarcasm, irony, rough lines, vivid colors, unique cartoon style.

His work ranges from digital to physical creation, which includes animation, graphic design, plywood installations, murals and comics art. Pijin is an urban character of a city bird who leaves traces after himself.
