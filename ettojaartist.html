ETTOJA

Artur Shirin, known as EttoJa, is a Lithuanian street‑artist and muralist whose work blends bold colors, geometric forms, and playful yet thought-provoking imagery. Trained in architecture and urbanism, he designs murals that integrate with the cityscape, creating public art that interacts naturally with its environment.

His pieces explore themes like urban life, social issues, and humanity’s relationship with nature and technology. Notable works include the “Jūroje” mural in Klaipėda’s “Aš nesu tik siena” street‑art project. EttoJa combines street‑art techniques with illustration and studio work, using his art to tell stories, provoke reflection, and transform urban spaces.
