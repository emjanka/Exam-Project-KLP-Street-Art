SHEPA

Shepa is a Lithuanian street art and graffiti artist from Klaipeda, known for transforming urban spaces with expressive murals. His work blends bold colors and dynamic forms, using graffiti techniques to convey emotion and identity while integrating with the city environment.

Being known does not always mean being recognized and valued, a lot of his artworks, exploring various life and sci-fi issues and thoughts, are still not seen at all.
